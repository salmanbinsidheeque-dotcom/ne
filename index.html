About Us

BZ Fitness Bodybuilding and Fitness Centre has been operating since 2016, bringing over seven years of expertise in health and fitness. Launched in Kuwait, it has quickly become one of the country’s fastest-growing fitness brands, dedicated to helping individuals stay fit using the most advanced training techniques.

In a short span, BZ Fitness has expanded to over six branches across Kuwait. Beyond offering top-tier bodybuilding and fitness services, the centre has diversified its operations—launching a fitness equipment manufacturing division and an e-commerce platform in 2022 to cater to growing customer demand.
